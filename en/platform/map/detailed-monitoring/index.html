Detailed Monitoring #

1. Favorite / Disfavour #

  • Favorite: To find quickly and conveniently track vehicle details that need attention and need to be handled.

    • Step 1: Click on the device.

    • Step 2: Click on in the device info box. The icon will change to .

  • Disfavour: If you have finished processing or do not prioritize the vehicle, you can un-favor it.

    • Step 1: Click on the device.

    • Step 2: Click on in the device info box. Once clicked, the message “Unliked” will appear. The icon will change to .

2. Monitor #

To track the details of that vehicle, location, distance, sensors today to the current time, do the following:

  • Way 1 : Click on car icon to view.
  • Way 2 : Cick on tab Monitor of that vehicle.

3. History trips #

To review the route of each vehicle, do one of the following:

  • Way 1: Click on the car icon.

  • Way 2: Click on tab History trips the final list of information of that vehicle.

  • Way 3: Click on > History trips of that device.

  • Way 4: Click in **Device info
    **.

After doing 1 of the 4 ways above, click on the tab Route as shown below:

3.1. Route #

  • To see the route, the distance that the car has traveled, perform the following steps:

    • Step 1 : Select the time to watch. Click OK.

    • Step 2 : The map screen now updates the route the vehicle runs during that time period.

    • Step 3: Blue circle : The road that the car runs.

    • Step 4 : Numbers in red squares : The points where the car stops.

    • Step 5 : Summarize driving time, stopping time, distance in that period.

3.2. Parameters on the chart #

3.2.1. Data log #

  • Step 1:

    • Click icon / to view/stop route playback.

    • Drag icon –—– to watch until a certain time as you like.

    • x1 : Choose whether to view fast/slow forward. Are from x0.5 -> x24.

  • Step 2 : When rewinding will display detailed data every second corresponding to that time in tab Data log.

  • Step 3 : Wherever the rewind line goes, it will display the speed, the total distance from the starting point to the running place. The route will be displayed in red indicating the route review.

  • Step 4 : Click it if you want to display more detailed data lines on 1 page (Can choose from 6 lines - 100 lines/page).

  • Step 5 : Downloadable in Excel format.

3.2.2. Speed #

  • 1. Select speed. Drag and hold the mouse to the end of the segment to zoom in to see the vehicle speed. Hovering over each point on the graph will show the current time and speed.

  • 2. Zoom out the gap in the chart.

  • 3. The label shows the time, distance, address, at that time.

  • 4. The speed and time are within the distance the vehicle is traveling.

3.2.3. GPS/GSM #

Satellite device number / Cell phone base to find the device’s location.

3.2.4. Battery / Power #

Number of batteries, number of sources (V) of the device.

3.2.5. Sensor #

Temperature/oil sensor attached to the unit.
