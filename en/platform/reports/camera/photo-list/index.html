List photos #

  • To view the photo history report, do the following:
    • Click on Report > Camera > List photos.

1. Search #

  • Step 1: You can search by the following criteria:

    • Date: Select the time you want to view the image.
    • Number plate: Select the device to view.
    • Camera: Select the camera you want to view (select one or more cameras).

  • Step 2:

    • Click Search to find results.
    • Click Reset to reset to default.

2. Detail #

  • 1: Click on the image to see detailed information.
    • Camera channel.
    • Photography time.

  • 2: Details:
    • Device name.
    • Camera channel.
    • Photography time.
    • Photo location.
    • Image location on the map.

3. Enlarge #

  • When searching for results, you can click on the image to see the image in a larger size.

  • Click on or click outside the image area to exit.
